V.2.5 Edit File Datasheet
In the datasheet all properties displayed. Read-only properties are inactive; For example, CAD features, such as weight or dimensions, which are automatically determined in the CAD system, should not be changed in the data sheet.
 
Protected files (checked out or released) will display of datasheet, but the OK button will not be active.

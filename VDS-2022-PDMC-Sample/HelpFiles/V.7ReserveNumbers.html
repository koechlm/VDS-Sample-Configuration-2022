V.7 Reserve Numbers
Vault Help - Best Practice - recommends to use VDS Numbering Reserve Tool.  http://help.autodesk.com/view/VAULT/2017/ENU/?guid=GUID-39D2F2F2-4F7B-4450-8FC4-EF988B30290B  
This extension gives you the ability to create / consume a range of numbers to configure:
  • iPart / iAssembly tables
  • Content Center Family components
  • …
The dialog and the logic behind is part of Quickstart 2017 environment.
